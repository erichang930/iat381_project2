    The National

    Trouble Will Find Me, The National’s new album, which is the sixth studio album for the Brooklyn band, and follows 2010’s critical and commercial success High Violet. The album is the most self-assured collection of songs produced by The National in its 14-year career. Trouble Will Find Me possesses a directness, a coherency and an approachability that suggests The National are at their most confident.
